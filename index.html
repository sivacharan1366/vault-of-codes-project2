🍫 Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 45 Minutes

Servings: 4 People

45:00

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, cocoa powder and baking powder.
  2. Add sugar and mix well.
  3. Add eggs, milk and vanilla extract.
  4. Stir until smooth batter forms.
  5. Pour batter into baking pan.
  6. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes.
  7. Let it cool and serve.
